Welcome to Cantre'r Gwaleod!

This is a quick practice mod that adds the mythical sunken kingdom of Cantre'r Gwaleod to Wales in CK3.

Thanks to:
 - Wikipedia, for general information on the myth.
    - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cantre%27r_Gwaelod
 - Llyfrgell Genedlaethol Cymru, the National Library of Wales, for information on the Black Book of Carmarthen.
    - https://www.library.wales/discover-learn/digital-exhibitions/manuscripts/the-middle-ages/the-black-book-of-carmarthen
